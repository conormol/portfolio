Welcome!

I am an engineer and developer with skills and expeirence in the energy industry, data analysis, and frontend development. I graduated with a Master's degree in energy systems engineering from University College Dublin in 2019, before spending a little over two years working as an engineer and analyst at Intel, based in Leixlip, Ireland. I spent the first half of 2022 working and living in hostels around the coast of Spain and Portugal, before returning to Ireland to upskill as a developer. When I'm not working, I play guitar in a hardcore band called LaVein, and produce electronic music under the alias Papal State. Feel free to look at some of my projects below, or contact me if you wish to get in touch.
